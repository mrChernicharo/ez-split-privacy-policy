Privacy Policy for EZSplit - AI Photo Bill Split

Last Updated: July 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how EZSplit ("we," "our," or "us") handles your information when you use our mobile application, "EZSplit - AI Photo Bill Split" (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Bill Images: When you take a picture of a restaurant bill, this image is temporarily sent to our servers for immediate OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and AI processing to extract product names, quantities, and prices. These images are processed and then immediately discarded; they are NOT stored or retained on our servers after processing.
  • Participant Names: Names you enter for the people splitting the bill. This data is processed locally on your device unless you choose to create an account and save bill history.
  • Bill Edits and Allocations: Any modifications you make to how products are split among participants. This data is processed locally on your device unless you choose to create an account and save bill history.
  • Account Information (Optional): Creating an account is entirely optional and not required to use the main features of the App. If you choose to create an account, we collect only your name and email address. This allows you to save bill history and access it across devices.
  • In-App Purchase Information: Details related to your purchases within the app. Please note that payment processing is handled by third-party payment processors (e.g., Google Play Store), and we do not directly store your credit card or payment details.
  • Feedback and Support: If you contact us for support or provide feedback, we collect the information you provide in your communication to help us address your query.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically (Non-Personal)

  • Device Information: We may collect non-identifying information about the device you use to access the App, such as the device model and operating system version. This helps us ensure app compatibility and performance.
  • Usage Data (Aggregated & Anonymized): We collect aggregated and anonymized information about how the App is used, such as features accessed and general usage patterns. This data cannot be used to identify you personally and helps us understand overall app performance and areas for improvement.
  • Crash Logs: If the App crashes, we may collect crash reports containing technical information about the crash (e.g., software version, device type). These reports are anonymized and used solely to diagnose and fix problems, improving app stability.
  • Advertising Identifiers: We may collect advertising identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID) for the purpose of displaying relevant advertisements within the App. These identifiers are not linked to your personal identity. You can reset or limit the use of these identifiers through your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the App: To process bill images for OCR and AI analysis, facilitate bill splitting, and deliver the core functionality of the App.
  • To Improve the App: To understand how the app is used (through aggregated, anonymized data) to enhance features, troubleshoot issues, and optimize performance.
  • For Account Management (Optional): If you create an account, your name and email are used to manage your account and allow you to save and access your bill history.
  • For Communication: To respond to your support inquiries and feedback.
  • For Advertising: To display advertisements within the App.
  • To Process In-App Purchases: To facilitate and manage your purchases within the App.
  • For Security and Fraud Prevention: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraudulent activities, or violations of our terms of service.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

3. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only in limited circumstances:

  • With Other Participants (User Initiated): When you finalize a bill split, you have the option to share the breakdown with other participants via messaging apps or other platforms. This sharing is initiated and controlled by you.
  • With Service Providers: We may share information with third-party vendors, consultants, and other service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, OCR/AI processing (for bill images, which are then discarded), and analytics. These third parties are obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes for which it was provided.
  • For Advertising: We may share non-personal data (like advertising identifiers) with third-party advertising partners to display ads within the App. These partners may use this information to provide personalized advertisements.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order or government agency).
  • Business Transfers: In connection with, or during negotiations of, any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business by another company.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with your explicit consent or at your direction.

4. Data Retention

By default, EZSplit does not retain your bill images or bill split data after processing. Once a bill image is processed by our servers for OCR and AI, it is immediately discarded. If you do not create an account, all bill-related data (participant names, splits) is processed and stored only locally on your device and is not retained by us.

If you choose to create an optional account, we will retain your name and email address for as long as your account is active to provide you with access to saved bill history and other account-related features. You can delete your account at any time.

We retain anonymized usage data and crash logs for a period necessary to analyze trends and improve the App's performance.

5. Your Choices and Rights

  • Account Deletion: If you have created an account, you can request the deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.
  • Opt-Out of Personalized Ads: You can often limit personalized ads through your device settings (e.g., by resetting your advertising ID or enabling "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android). Please note that even if you opt out of personalized ads, you may still see non-personalized ads.
  • Permissions: You can control the permissions the App has on your device (e.g., camera access) through your device settings.
